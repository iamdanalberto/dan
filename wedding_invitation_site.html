Our Love Story

We met on a rainy Tuesday afternoon at the local coffee shop downtown. Sarah was reading her favorite novel while Michael was struggling with his laptop nearby. When his coffee accidentally spilled over her book, what started as an embarrassing moment turned into hours of conversation and laughter.

Three years later, on the very same rainy Tuesday, Michael proposed at that same coffee shop with the same book (now coffee-stained and treasured) in hand. Sarah said yes before he could even finish asking the question!

We can't wait to begin this new chapter of our love story with all of you by our side.
